Hi, and welcome to my portfolio. Everything that you see I created while attending Austin Coding Academy's Full-Stack JavaScript Web Development course.

I've been interested in web development since I was in college studying at Texas State University. While I did not get my bachelor's degree in web development, I did have a digital media concentration. This allowed me to take a into to development course, which is where my interest started. Our final project was to build our own website, which I really enjoyed. I like putting things together and figuring out why they fit, which I think is part of the reason that I enjoy coding. I also like the creative aspect that front-end developing allows.

I found Austin Coding Academy when I decided that I wanted to switch careers and give web development a shot. I'm glad that I enjoy challenges because there certainly have been a lot, but it is such a great feeling when you finally figure out what you were missing, even if you spent a long time looking at your code and it was a letter 's'. I am excited for the future and to see where web development takes me. You can continue scrolling to find my portfolio of projects I worked on while at Austin Coding Academy.
